Howdy!

My name is Justin West. I’m a creative director and brand strategist focused on building brands with real meaning behind them. I currently work as a Brand Specialist at Door Controls USA, where I help shape how the company shows up across digital, print, and product.

Over the years, I’ve worked across agencies, nonprofits, and higher education, which has given me a practical perspective on creative work. I’m not just thinking about how something looks. I’m thinking about how it actually works and how it supports the bigger picture.I’ve built brand systems, managed websites and digital platforms, and created content across print and online. I also spent time teaching graphic design at the college level, which helped me get better at explaining ideas and keeping things clear.

At the end of the day, I care about making work that feels honest, holds up over time, and does what it’s supposed to do.
